App Name: Eye Focus Timer
Subtitle: 20-20-20 Rule & Eye Exercises

Description:
Eye Focus Timer is your personal screen wellness toolkit built around the 20-20-20 rule, recommended by the American Academy of Ophthalmology (AAO). Every 20 minutes of screen time, take a 20-second break and look at something 20 feet away — a simple habit for healthier screen use.

SMART 20-20-20 TIMER
Start the timer and focus on your work with confidence. Eye Focus Timer runs quietly in the background, counting down your chosen interval — 20, 30, 45, or 60 minutes. When it's time for a break, you'll receive a gentle notification with optional haptic feedback and sound. A beautiful 20-second countdown guides your break, reminding you to look at a distant object. After each break, the timer automatically resets so you maintain your routine throughout your workday.

SIX INTERACTIVE EYE EXERCISES
Go beyond simple breaks with exercises based on established optometric guidelines (AAO, AOA) to support your visual comfort:

Follow the Dot — A glowing dot moves across your screen in smooth patterns. Track it with your eyes without moving your head. A common optometric exercise for eye muscle coordination, recommended by the AAO.

Near-Far Focus — Alternate your gaze between a near target (your thumb at 15cm) and a far target on screen. Each focus hold lasts 3 seconds with haptic cues. Based on accommodation exercises recommended by the American Optometric Association (AOA).

Palming Rest — Cover your eyes with warm palms while following a guided breathing animation. A traditional relaxation technique referenced in optometric wellness literature.

Figure 8 Trace — Follow a dot as it traces an infinity (∞) pattern. A common optometric exercise for eye muscle flexibility and binocular coordination, described in AOA guidelines.

Blink Break — Guided rapid-blink exercise with visual cues. Research suggests blink rate decreases significantly during screen use (Tsubota et al., NEJM 1993). This exercise encourages a healthy blink rate.

Peripheral Peek — Stare at the center fixation point while dots flash at the edges of your screen. Tap when you spot one. An awareness exercise based on standard visual field concepts from optometric practice.

HABIT TRACKING
Monitor your screen wellness habits with statistics. See your daily Eye Health Score — a habit-tracking metric calculated from your breaks, exercises, and streak consistency (not a clinical measurement). Track weekly trends with visual bar charts. Build daily streaks to develop lasting healthy screen habits.

CUSTOMIZABLE EXPERIENCE
Choose your preferred break interval from 20 to 60 minutes. Toggle push notifications, sound effects, and haptic feedback independently. All preferences are saved automatically between sessions.

BASED ON ESTABLISHED GUIDELINES
The 20-20-20 rule is recommended by the American Academy of Ophthalmology (AAO) and the American Optometric Association (AOA) as a preventive measure for digital eye strain. The eye exercises in this app are based on established optometric principles described in peer-reviewed literature (see Sources below). This app helps you build healthy screen habits based on these guidelines.

PRIVACY FIRST
No account required. No data collection. No cloud sync. Everything stays on your device. Your health data belongs to you alone.

Whether you're a developer, designer, writer, gamer, or anyone who spends significant time looking at screens, Eye Focus Timer gives you the tools to build healthy screen habits.
